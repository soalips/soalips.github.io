25. bantu jawab kk soal fisika kelas 10 smk​


Jawaban:

Soal Tipe A

massa benda tersebut adalah 3 kg

[tex]\Sigma F_x = ma\\F = ma\\15 = 5m\\m = 3 \; kg[/tex]

Soal Tipe B

JIka C :

massa = 500 gr = 0,5 kg

[tex]\Sigma F_y = 0\\ N - mg = 0\\N = mg\\N = 0,5(10)\\N = 5 \; N[/tex]

[tex]\Sigma F_x = ma\\F = ma\\a = \frac{20}{0,5}\\a = 40 \; m/s^2[/tex]

Jika D :

[tex]\Sigma F_x = ma\\F \cos 60 = ma\\20 \frac{1}{2} = ma\\a = \frac{10}{0,5}\\a = 20 m/s^2[/tex]

Soal Tipe C

Gaya normal benda N = mg = 10.10 = 100 N

N > F , maka benda masih tetap diam.

[tex]\Sigma F_x = 0\\F - fg = 0\\fg = 30 \; N[/tex]

karena benda masih diam, maka percepatannya juga 0

Soal Tipe D

[tex]\Sigma F_x = ma\\mg\sin 60 = ma\\a = g \sin 60\\a = 10 \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\\a = 5 \sqrt{2} \; m/s^2[/tex]
