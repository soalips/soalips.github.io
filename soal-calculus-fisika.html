35. CALCULUS MATH QUIZ ! BERIKAN PEMBAHASAN YG MENURUN & EKSAK YAAA SOBAT ! SEMANGAT ! #CALCULUSkuyy!! #MATH.IS.FUN​


[tex]\sf Hasil\ dari\ \int\limits{\frac{\cos^4x(1-\tan^4x)}{\cos 4x-1}} \, dx \ adalah\ \boxed{\frac{1}{8}\csc x\sec x+C}[/tex]

.

PEMBAHASAN

Integral merupakan anti turunan.

.

Sifat-sifat dari Integral Tak Tentu

[tex]\int\limits {} \, dx=x+C\\\\\int\limits {k} \, dx = kx+C\\\\\int\limits {x^n} \, dx = \frac{1}{n+1}x^n+C,\ n\neq -1\\\\\int\limits {kf(x)} \, dx = k \int\limits {f(x)} \, dx \\\\\int\limits {[f(x)+g(x)]} \, dx = \int\limits {f(x)} \, dx + \int\limits {g(x)} \, dx \\\\\int\limits {[f(x)-g(x)]} \, dx = \int\limits {f(x)} \, dx - \int\limits {g(x)} \, dx \\\\\int\limits {a^x} \, dx = (\frac{1}{\ln a})a^x+C \\\\\int\limits {a^{u(x)}} \, dx = (\frac{1}{u'(x)\ln a})a^{u(x)} + C[/tex]

[tex]\int\limits {\frac{1}{x}} \, dx = \ln |x| +C\\\\\int\limits {\frac{1}{u(x)}} \, dx = \frac{1}{u'x}\ln |u(x)| + C\\\\\int\limits {e^x} \, dx = e^x + C\\\\\int\limits {e^{u(x)}} \, dx = \frac{1}{u'x}e^{u(x)}+C[/tex]

.

DIKETAHUI

[tex]\int\limits{\frac{\cos^4x(1-\tan^4x)}{\cos 4x-1}} \, dx[/tex]

.

DITANYA

Tentukan nilai integralnya !

.

PENYELESAIAN

[tex]\int\limits{\frac{\cos^4x(1-\tan^4x)}{\cos 4x-1}} \, dx \\\\= \int\limits{\frac{\cos^4x-\sin^4x}{\cos 4x-1}} \, dx \\\\= \int\limits{\frac{(\frac{1}{2}+\frac{1}{2}\cos 2x)^2-(\frac{1}{2}-\frac{1}{2}\cos 2x)^2}{(1-2\sin^22x)-1}} \, dx\\\\= \int\limits{\frac{\frac{1}{4}+\frac{1}{2}\cos 2x+\frac{1}{4}\cos^2 2x-(\frac{1}{4}-\frac{1}{2}\cos 2x+\frac{1}{4}\cos^2 2x)}{(1-2\sin^22x)-1}} \, dx[/tex]

[tex]= -\int\limits{\frac{\frac{1}{4}+\frac{1}{2}\cos 2x+\frac{1}{4}\cos^2 2x-\frac{1}{4}+\frac{1}{2}\cos 2x-\frac{1}{4}\cos^2 2x)}{2\sin^22x}} \, dx\\\\= -\int\limits{\frac{\cos 2x}{2\sin^22x}} \, dx\\\\= -\frac{1}{2}\int\limits{\frac{\cot 2x}{\sin 2x}} \, dx \\\\= -\frac{1}{2}\int\limits {\cot 2x\csc 2x} \, dx \\\\[/tex]

.

Mencari Integral

[tex]\int\limits {\cot 2x\csc 2x} \, dx[/tex]

Misal :

[tex]u=\csc 2x[/tex]

[tex]du = -2\cot 2x\csc 2x\\\\-\frac{1}{2} du= \cot 2x\csc 2x[/tex]

.

Sehingga

[tex]\int\limits {\cot 2x\csc 2x} \, dx\\\\= \int\limits {-\frac{1}{2}} \, du \\\\= -\frac{1}{2}u+C\\\\= -\frac{1}{2}\csc 2x + C[/tex]

.

Maka

[tex]\int\limits{\frac{\cos^4x(1-\tan^4x)}{\cos 4x-1}} \, dx \\\\= -\frac{1}{2}\int\limits {\cot 2x\csc 2x} \, dx \\\\= -\frac{1}{2} (-\frac{1}{2}\csc x+C)\\\\= \frac{1}{4} \csc 2x+C \\\\= \frac{1}{4} \frac{1}{\sin 2x} +C\\\\= \frac{1}{4} \frac{1}{2\sin x\cos x}+C \\\\= \frac{1}{8} \csc x\sec x+C[/tex]

.

[tex]\sf Jadi,\ hasil\ dari\ \int\limits{\frac{\cos^4x(1-\tan^4x)}{\cos 4x-1}} \, dx \ adalah\ \boxed{\frac{1}{8}\csc x\sec x+C}[/tex]
