8. How to make an pake bahasa Inggris​


Jawaban:

how to make instan noodles

ingredients :

- 1 pack indomie instan noodles

- pot

- scissors

- bowl or plate

- fork

- stove

- water

- egg and chesee ( can use or not)

steps :

- first put the stove and fill the water in the pot

- Second, wait for the water in the pot to boil

- while waiting for it to boil, we put the indomie seasoning into a bowl or seasoning plate consisting of oil, sauce, soy sauce, and seasoning powder

- after boiling we put the noodles into the pot then wait about 3 minutes while stirring

- after the noodles are cooked, we drain them and then pour the noodles into a bowl or plate that is already filled with spices

- after that stir until evenly add the fried onions

- after that you can also add cheese or eggs (you can use it or not)

- noodle food ready to eat
